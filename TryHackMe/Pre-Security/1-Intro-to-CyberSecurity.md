**Overview**

Understanding the what is Offensive and Defensive Security means and explore about career opportunities in the Cyber.



**What I Learned**



**Offensive Security**

* Offensive security involves attacking systems

&#x20;  legally in order to find vulnerabilities before hackers do

* Also known as ethical hacking or pentesting
* The aim - to discover flaws before hackers do



**Deffensive Security**

• Defensive security protects systems

against attacks

• SOC (Security Operations Centre)monitors systems 24/7

• SIEM (Security Information and Event Management) 

collects and analyzes security logs

• Digital forensics investigates security incidents

• Threat intelligence monitors known threats



**careers in Cyber**

* &#x20;SOC Analyst - system monitoring and security defense
* &#x20;Penetration Tester - ethical hacking of systems
* &#x20;Security Engineer - building secure systems
* &#x20;Digital Forensics - forensic investigations
* &#x20;Malware Analyst - malicious code analysis



**Conclusion**

The concepts of cybersecurity have dual nature – offensive and

defensive. Both concepts collaborate to defend systems and data from real world attacks.
