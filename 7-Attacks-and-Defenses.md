**# Module 7 - Attacks and Defenses**



**Overview**

Learn how attackers and defenders think, how

data is protected and how real world systems

are attacked and defended in practice.



**What I Learned**



**How Attackers Think**

* &#x20;Reconnaissance - gather information about target
* &#x20;Scanning - find open ports and vulnerabilities
* &#x20;Exploitation - attack the found weaknesses
* &#x20;Post exploitation - move deeper into system
* &#x20;Covering tracks - hide evidence of attack



**Common Attack Types**

* &#x20;Phishing - trick users into giving credentials
* &#x20;Malware - malicious software installed on system
* &#x20;Ransomware - encrypts files and demands payment
* &#x20;SQL Injection - attack databases through input
* &#x20;XSS - inject malicious JavaScript into websites
* &#x20;DoS/DDoS - overwhelm systems with traffic
* &#x20;Man in the Middle - intercept communications



**How Defenders Think**

* &#x20;Prevention - stop attacks before they happen
* &#x20;Detection - identify attacks in progress
* &#x20;Response - contain and fix active attacks
* &#x20;Recovery - restore systems after attack



**Defense Tools and Techniques**

* &#x20;Firewall - filters incoming and outgoing traffic
* &#x20;Antivirus - detects and removes malware
* &#x20;SIEM - collects and analyses security logs
* &#x20;IDS/IPS - detects and prevents intrusions
* &#x20;Encryption - protects data in transit and rest
* &#x20;Multi Factor Authentication - adds extra login security



**Data Protection**

* &#x20;Encryption protects sensitive data
* &#x20;Backups allow recovery after attacks
* &#x20;Access control limits who can access data
* &#x20;Patch management fixes known vulnerabilities



**Summary**

Understanding both attacker and defender

mindsets is crucial for a Defense Analyst role.

This module directly connects to my goal of

working in a SOC environment detecting and

responding to real world cyber threats.
